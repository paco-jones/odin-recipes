Vegan Mac and Cheese

This is my third favorite food.

Ingredients

  • Box of vegan mac and cheese
  • Sauce pan
  • Margarine
  • Almond milk

Steps

  1. Open Vegan Mac and Cheese Box
  2. Bring 8oz of water to boil in sauce pan
  3. Reduce to simmer, add dry macaroni noodles
  4. Cook for 15 minutes
  5. Add cheese sauce when noodles are done
  6. Serve on plate or in bowl
