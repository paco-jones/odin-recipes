Bowl of Chex

This is my favorite food. It is easy, simple and quick. Anytime I run out of cereal, I feel like the entire world is wrong. Cereal is good. You should eat some.

Ingredients

  • Chex Ceraal 8 0z
  • Almond Milk 5 oz
  • Bowl
  • Spoon

    • Steps

    1. Open cereal box
    2. Shake milk container before opening
    3. Pour cereal into bowl
    4. Pour almond milk on cereal
    5. Eat with spoon
