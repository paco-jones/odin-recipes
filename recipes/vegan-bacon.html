Vegan Bacon

Vegan Bacon

This is my fourth favorite food. While it is easy, it does take more work than my other recommendations.

Ingredients

  • Tempeh 8oz
  • Soy sauce
  • Plate

    • Steps

    1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
    2. Cut tempeh into small strips
    3. Soak in soy sauce
    4. Cook for 35 minutes
    5. Enjoy hot
