Vegan Nuggets

This is my second favorite food. It is easy, simple and quick like cereal.

Ingredients

  • Box of vegan nuggets
  • Plate
  • Barbeque Sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Open nuggets box
  3. Put nuggets on cookie sheet and place in oven
  4. Cook for 35 minutes
  5. Place and plate and dip into barbeque sauce
